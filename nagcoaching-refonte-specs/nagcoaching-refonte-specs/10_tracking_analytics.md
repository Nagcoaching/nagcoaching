# 10 — Tracking & analytics (GA4 / Ads)

## Conversions principales
- `lead_form_submit` (formulaire envoyé)
- `click_to_call` (clic téléphone)
- `whatsapp_click` (si bouton)
- `book_appointment` (Calendly / réservation)
- `directions_click` (clic itinéraire Google Maps)

## Tagging
- Google Tag Manager recommandé
- GA4 + Google Ads conversion linking
- Meta Pixel (si campagnes IG/FB)

## UTM
- Standardiser : utm_source / utm_medium / utm_campaign / utm_content

## KPIs dashboard
- Leads / semaine
- Conversion rate (visites → lead)
- CPL (Ads)
- Top pages d’entrée (SEO local)
